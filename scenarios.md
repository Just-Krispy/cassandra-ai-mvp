# Cassandra AI - Copy & Example Scenarios

## Example Scenarios (Pre-Written, Ready to Test)

### Scenario 1: Job Decision
**Prompt:**
"I have two job offers. Job A: $150K at Google (stable, good benefits, slow career growth). Job B: $120K at Series A startup (0.5% equity, fast growth, risky). I'm 28, single, no debt. Which should I choose?"

**Use Case:** Career decisions with clear trade-offs (stability vs. upside)

---

### Scenario 2: Business Launch Timing
**Prompt:**
"I've been building a SaaS product for 6 months. It's 80% done. Should I launch now to get early feedback, or wait 3 more months to reach 100% feature completeness? Competitors are moving fast."

**Use Case:** Timing decisions with competitive pressure

---

### Scenario 3: Investment Strategy
**Prompt:**
"I have $10,000 to invest. Should I put it in an S&P 500 index fund (stable, 10% annual return historically) or Bitcoin (volatile, potential 50%+ returns but could also crash)? I'm 35, moderate risk tolerance."

**Use Case:** Risk/reward financial decisions

---

### Scenario 4: Geopolitical Risk
**Prompt:**
"Will China invade Taiwan in the next 5 years? I'm considering investing in Taiwan semiconductor companies. What's the probability and what factors should I watch?"

**Use Case:** High-stakes predictions with geopolitical complexity

---

### Scenario 5: Career Relocation
**Prompt:**
"Should I relocate from Ohio to San Francisco for a 30% pay increase ($100K → $130K)? Cost of living is 80% higher. I'm early in my career (25), want to build network in tech."

**Use Case:** Lifestyle vs. financial decisions with long-term implications
